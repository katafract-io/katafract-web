What SafeOpen Is

SafeOpen is a QR code and link inspection tool. It decodes QR codes, classifies payloads, and scores link risk using local, on-device algorithms. It is a utility to inform your decision before opening a link — not a security guarantee, not a VPN, and not a browser.

No Warranty on Risk Scores

SafeOpen’s risk scores are produced by deterministic local heuristics. They are informational only and do not constitute a guarantee of safety. A link scored “Likely Safe” may still be harmful; a link scored “High Risk” may be entirely benign. You are responsible for your own judgment about whether to open any link. Katafract accepts no liability for harm resulting from links you choose to open or not open based on SafeOpen’s output.
