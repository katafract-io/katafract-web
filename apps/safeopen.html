How It Works

Scan. Inspect. Open safely.

01

Scan or Paste

Point the camera at a QR code or barcode, paste a link, or share one from any app. SafeOpen decodes it instantly — no internet required at this step.

02

Inspect

SafeOpen classifies the payload type, normalizes the URL, strips tracking parameters, and runs local risk heuristics. You see the destination, the clean URL, the risk level, and a plain-language explanation of every flag.

03

Decide

Copy the clean URL, open directly in Safari, or tap Open Safely (Pro) to browse through a Katafract proxy node — redirect chain preview first, then an isolated browser session with a disposable IPv6 identity.

🔒 Designed to stay on your device

Free tier inspection runs entirely on-device. Scanning, decoding, risk scoring, tracking parameter detection — no network calls, no URL submissions, no servers involved. Your scans are stored locally and never leave your phone.

Pro tier connects to Katafract proxy nodes only when you explicitly tap “Open Safely.” The request routes through our infrastructure under a fresh disposable identity. We do not log your browsing activity or store the URLs you open.

Apple Privacy Nutrition Label: Data Not Collected (free tier). Pro tier: IP Address only when actively routing an open request — never tied to your identity.
